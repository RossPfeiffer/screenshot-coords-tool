🎯 Screenshot Coordinates Tool

Instructions:

• Click anywhere on the image to get pixel coordinates

• Red crosshairs will appear at your click position

• Coordinates will appear in the top-right corner

• Use these coordinates for automation commands like [!click x,y]

• Press Ctrl+C to copy coordinates to clipboard

• Crosshairs are made with div elements, not cursor!

Coordinates:
X: 0
Y: 0
Commands:
Left: [!click:0,0]
Right: [!rclick:0,0]
Middle: [!mclick:0,0]
